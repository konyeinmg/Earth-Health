What is Ozone Layer?

Ozone is a colourless gas which is found in the atmosphere of our upper atmosphere. The ozone layer is a layer of the earth's upper atmosphere that is characterized by high ozone content which blocks most of the sun's ultraviolet radiation from entering the lower atmosphere. The ozone Layer is found between the stratosphere and the troposhere. The ozone layer contains less than 10 parts per million of ozone, while the average ozone concentration in Earth's atmosphere as a whole is about 0.3 parts per million. The ozone layer is mainly in the lower portion of the stratosphere,from approximately 20 to 30 kilometers(12 to 9 mi) above Earth,although its thickness varies seasinally and geographically. The ozone layer filters out and converts UV ligth into heat energy-this makes it a higher temperature than other parts of the upper atmosphere. The layer of ozone gas is what which protect us from the harmful ultraviolet radiations of the sun. The ozone layer absorbs these harmful radiations and thus prevents these raus from entering the earth's atmosphere.
